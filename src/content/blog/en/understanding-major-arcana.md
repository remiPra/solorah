The **22 major arcana** of the Tarot de Marseille are the most powerful cards in the deck. From The Fool to The World, each arcana tells a stage of the soul's initiatory journey.

## What is a major arcana?

The word "arcana" comes from the Latin *arcanum*, meaning "secret." The major arcana represent life's great lessons, turning points, and the deep forces that move through us.

## The 22 arcana at a glance

- **The Fool (0)** — Freedom, journey, the unknown
- **The Magician (I)** — Beginnings, creative potential
- **The High Priestess (II)** — Intuition, inner wisdom
- **The Empress (III)** — Creativity, abundance
- **The Emperor (IV)** — Structure, authority
- **The Hierophant (V)** — Guidance, teaching
- **The Lovers (VI)** — Choice, love
- **The Chariot (VII)** — Victory, determination

## How to interpret them?

During a reading, a major arcana draws attention to a central theme in your life. It's not about predicting the future, but about **illuminating** what's happening within you.

> Each card is a mirror. What you see in it depends on what you carry within.
